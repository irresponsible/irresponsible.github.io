About

The irresponsible clojure guild (founded 2015) is a community-run organisation that provides high quality clojure libraries.

Initially we wrote all of the libraries ourselves, but these days we've adopted some libraries that were not being well maintained by other people.

In the near future we will diversify into installable applications too. Keep an eye out for that!
